Before diving into technical details, complete these prerequisites:

### 1. Explore the BitBadges Platform

Familiarize yourself with the platform by exploring [bitbadges.io](https://bitbadges.io):

-   Review the landing and explore pages
-   Complete the collection creation flow
-   Examine transaction JSONs by clicking "Show Tx" at the end of different creation flows
